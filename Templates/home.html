About

Have you ever watched a movie and ended up disappointed with the outcome? Our project focused on designing models to predict IMDB movie ratings based on genre, year of release, budget, duration, and director. We tested four machine learning models and visualized our data through the use of Tableau.

No longer do we have to waste time, money, and energy watching a less than inspiring movie. Browse our website for more details!
